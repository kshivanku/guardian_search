Thanks for checking "First First" out. This tool lets you search all the articles written by Guardian since 1803 on something or someone. Use the big search bar on top to find Guardian articles on any topic.

Here are some tips to search:

  • Put quotes if you want to search for a phrase, for example "Donald Trump"
  • You can use AND, OR and NOT operators to make compound queries. For example "Climate Change" AND Obama
